📢 A Message to Parents Regarding Their Children's Education

🎯 With 30+ years in computer education, I want to share key insights to help parents guide their children in an AI-driven world.

🧭 10 Key Messages

1. 🏭 Think Industry, Not Subjects

Choose subjects based on the future industry your child is likely to join.

2. 💻 Computer Literacy is Now Basic Literacy

A person with basic computer skills stands far ahead of the uneducated and not far behind the highly educated.

3. 🤖 Understand the AI Era

  • AI is no longer optional — every decision about careers must now include its impact.
  • The AI revolution is ongoing — it’s expanding fast and will define the next decade.
  • AI is reshaping every industry — just like computers once did, but more deeply.
  • AI will create new roles — especially in automation, coding, and system design.

4. 🎓 Rethink Education for the AI Era

  • Subjects are shifting from theory to practical: think Process Automation instead of Social Studies.
  • Education will be shorter, focused, and skill-based.
  • Degrees will matter less — real-world ability will matter more.

5. 🗣️ Learn to Learn & Soft Skills Matter

Adaptability, communication, curiosity, and emotional intelligence will outlast any technical trend.

6. 🌐 Online is the New Marketplace

No matter what you make and where you make it—you will sell it online.

7. 📊 Data Skills Are the New Math

Understanding and working with data is now a fundamental skill — as important as basic arithmetic once was.

8. 🧭 Career Paths Will Be Non-Linear

Success won't follow a straight path; careers will evolve, switch domains, and require constant reinvention.

9. 🎯 Domain Knowledge is Part of the Education

The knowledge of the industry that you want to make money from should be part of your education.

10. 🎯 Skills Speak Louder Than Degrees

In today's world, your ability to deliver results matters far more than your academic credentials alone.

💼 The Future Job Market

1. 🧾 End of Traditional Jobs

Long-term jobs with pensions are disappearing.

2. 📄 Rise of Contracts

Work will be short-term, task-based, and flexible.

3. 🧠 Highly Skilled Teams

Small, smart, technical teams will lead the way.

4. 🚚 Outsourcing Will Prevail

Whatever can be outsourced, will be outsourced.

5. 🏛️ Disappearing Government Jobs

Government jobs all over the world will pay less and offer fewer benefits in the future.
